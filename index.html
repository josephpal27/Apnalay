Welcome to Apnalay - a Home Where You Belong

Apnalay has been conceived, designed and constructed by the inner Wheel club of Central Calcutta. It is the result of combined efforts of more than 70 like-minded ladies who chose to undertake this service for the senior citizens of our society.

A very potent guidance came our way in the shape of Panchvati Home, New Delhi and its Founder-Trustee Ms. Neelam Mohan. Aided by their experience, Apnalay finally shaped its principles and ideologies.

Our Facilities

Apnalay provides comfortable living, 24/7 care, and engaging activities, ensuring safety and well-being for all residents.

Amazing Atmosphere

Experience a warm and inviting environment that fosters companionship and joy for all residents.

Assisted Living

Receive personalized support and care tailored to enhance your daily living while maintaining your independence.

Skilled Nursing

Access professional medical care and support from qualified nursing staff, ensuring health and well-being around the clock.

Recreational Activities

Enjoy a variety of engaging programs and events that promote social interaction, creativity, and physical well-being.

Pharmacy Support

Nutritional Care

Savor delicious, balanced meals tailored to meet individual dietary needs, promoting health and wellness every day.

We make a Difference in your lives

At Apnalay, we believe in fostering a strong sense of community among our residents. Through various social events, workshops, and group activities, we encourage connections and friendships that enrich daily life. Our residents can participate in volunteer opportunities and community outreach programs, allowing them to share their skills and experiences while making a positive impact.
