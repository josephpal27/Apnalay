Welcome to Apnalay - a Home Where You Belong

Apnalay is a thoughtfully designed senior citizen living home, founded by the Inner Wheel Club of Central Calcutta. Built through the dedication of over 70 compassionate women, Apnalay provides a safe, supportive, and enriching environment for elderly people.

Inspired by Panchvati Home in New Delhi, Apnalay blends comfort and companionship to enhance the quality of life for its residents. We offer a unique “Non Profit Pay & Stay” model, providing not just physical and emotional security, but also a lively community where seniors can thrive through the second innings of their lives.

Through engaging discussions, interactive activities, and recreational programs, Apnalay ensures that every resident enjoys a fulfilling life. Whether recovering from illness, staying temporarily while family members are away, or simply seeking a refreshing change, Apnalay is a place where seniors can live with dignity, joy, and purpose.

Our Gallery

Discover glimpses of life at Apnalay, where our vibrant spaces and joyful moments reflect the warmth and care our community offers every day.

Apnalay… a place where time slows down, hearts speak, and smiles heal. Tucked away in Thakurpukur, Kolkata, this beautiful old age home is more than just a shelter — it’s a haven of peace and comfort. With over 30 beautifully maintained and fully furnished rooms, Apnalay is thoughtfully designed to ensure the elderly live not just safely, but happily. The silence here isn’t empty… it’s filled with stories, blessings, and quiet strength. It’s a place that feels like home — even if it’s not the one they began their journey in. Spending time here reminded me that love, care, and respect can create magic — even in the most silent corners. Here’s to Apnalay — a home of dignity, love, and hope.
