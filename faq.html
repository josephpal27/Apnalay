Frequently asked questions (FAQ’s)

Find answers to common queries about out facilities and services.

Apnalay offers different types of rooms, including single and double-bed rooms, as well as spacious suites. Each room is fully furnished with amenities like air conditioning, TV, intercom, and Wi-Fi, ensuring comfort and convenience for all residents.

Residents enjoy nutritious meals, daily housekeeping, laundry services, 24/7 security, and access to medical care. Additional services like yoga sessions and transport can be arranged upon request.

Yes, Apnalay has a medical room for basic care and regular physician visits. For emergencies or specialized care, doctors, nurses, and physiotherapists are available on call.

Family members are always welcome to visit their loved ones at Apnalay. We have comfortable lounges and common areas where residents and visitors can spend quality time together.

Apnalay provides a range of activities to keep residents engaged and active, including interactive sessions, reading in our library, gardening on the terrace, and organized social events. We strive to create a lively and enjoyable environment for all our residents.

The safety of our residents is our top priority. We have 24/7 security surveillance, CCTV cameras, smoke detectors, and emergency call systems installed throughout the premises. Our staff is trained to respond promptly to any emergency.
