Hello, Friends,
Yesterday, we had a small program at Apnalay, performed by the children of Sanskriti Parichay, headed by Mrs. Shubha Churiwal. The children beautifully recited Bengali bhajans which our residents were singing along, Shlokas - the meaning was explained to us in English by the children and then they performed a hilarious skit on Tenali Raman. Everyone laughed and enjoyed the skit. It was a laughter therapy for our residents. It was a pleasure to dance with the residents at the end. A mother donated mangoes and some contributed funds for the food to be served to the residents. PP Sunita Jalan with both her children celebrated her birthday with the residents by cutting the cake along with Shashi bhabhi whose birthday also falls in the same month. The parents and grand parents of the children were eager and happy to see the whole Apnalay and found it very neat and clean.

Adding life to your years
